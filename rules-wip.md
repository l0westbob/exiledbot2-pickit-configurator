# Rules for items
---
## Magic
Magic Items must have a min of 1 affix and can hav up to 2 affix.
Magic Items can have a max of 1 prefix and 1 suffix.
Magic Items that only have 1 affix, have either a prefix or a suffix.

---
## Rare
You need a Magic Item with 2 affixes (1 prefix & 1 suffix) to make it a Rare Item with a Regal Orb
Rare Items can have 1 prefix and 3 suffix, but also 3 prefix and 1 suffix. Of course, all combinations in between.

This means:
1P2S, 1P3S, 1S2P, 1S3P
2P2S, 2P3S, 2S2P, 2S3P
3P2S, 3P3S, 3S2P, 3S3P

---

## Normal/Any
Normal Items, but also Magic and Rare ones, even unique ones can have an extra stat, which is available sockets. This needs to be implemented afterwards.
